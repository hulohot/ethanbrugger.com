This paper proposes a fast pixelated Optical Proximity Correction (OPC) approach based on nonlinear compressive sensing theory. The method downsamples layout patterns and applies iterative hard thresholding to optimize mask patterns for semiconductor lithography, reducing computational complexity while improving mask manufacturability and process windows.
