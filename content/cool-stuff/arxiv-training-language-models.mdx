title: "Training language models to follow instructions with human feedback"
description: A groundbreaking paper from OpenAI introducing RLHF (Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback) to align language models with human intent, enabling applications like ChatGPT.
date: "2022-03-22"
url: https://arxiv.org/abs/2203.02155
published: true
---

This paper presents InstructGPT, a method for training large language models to follow instructions and align with human preferences using reinforcement learning from human feedback (RLHF). The approach significantly improves the helpfulness, harmlessness, and honesty of language model outputs while reducing toxic content.
